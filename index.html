    About US

    Welcome to iConcern, where your concern is our concern!

    At iConcern, our mission is to empower individuals who are newly diagnosed with type 2 diabetes by helping them identify and address their immediate health concerns. We understand that navigating your health journey can be challenging, especially when it comes to articulating your worries, fears, questions, and needs to your primary care provider. That's where iConcern steps in.

    More About Us

    What We Do

    iConcern is a web-based tool designed to simplify the process of identifying and understanding your immediate health concerns regarding the management of your diabetes. Most often appointment times, the shock of diabetes diagnosis, and other factors make it challenging for people newly diagnosed with diabetes to communicate their concerns to their healthcare providers at appointments. Our innovative platform offers a personalized experience, guiding you through a series of questions and prompts to uncover what matters most to you right now. We also provide resources to help address the concerns, questions, fears, and needs that you may have regarding the effective management of your diabetes. Finally, we facilitate a conversation with your primary care provider to communicate your identified concerns for prompt evaluation and addressing. Whether you're facing a new diabetes diagnosis, uncertain about what to do after your diagnosis, have unaddressed concerns or worries, or simply seeking to improve your overall well-being, iConcern is here to support you.

    How it works

    Identify Your Concerns

    Our intuitive tool helps you pinpoint specific health concerns, providing clarity on what you need to discuss with your healthcare provider.

    Access Resources

    Based on your identified concerns, iConcern offers tailored resources, including articles, videos, and support tools to help you understand and manage your health. We also provide useful information and links to available community resources including information on diabetes specialist care services, exercise programs, nutritional programs, and diabetes educational programs in communities.

    Facilitate Conversations

    We know that discussing health concerns with your provider can be daunting. We provide practical tips and conversation starters to help you communicate effectively with your primary care provider, ensuring your concerns are heard and addressed.

    Our Commitment

    At iConcern, we are committed to fostering a supportive and empowering environment for all users. We believe that informed and proactive individuals can make better health decisions, leading to improved outcomes and a higher quality of life. Our tool is designed with you in mind, offering a user-friendly experience that respects your privacy and prioritizes your well-being. Join the iConcern community today and take the first step towards a more confident and informed approach to your health. Let us help you articulate your concerns and connect with the resources you need to thrive.
